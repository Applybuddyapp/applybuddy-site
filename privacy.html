Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 10, 2026

At **ApplyBuddy AI**, your privacy is not an afterthought—it is a core feature. We believe your career data is personal and should remain that way. This policy outlines exactly what we collect and how we protect it.

Information We Collect

Authentication: We use Google OAuth to securely sign you in. We only store your email address—never your password.

Usage Data: To enforce our daily limits, we track the number of AI requests made per account.

Transient Text: Job descriptions and resumes you paste are processed via OpenRouter/Anthropic to give you results. This text is processed in real-time and not permanently stored on our servers.

Our Privacy Commitments

We Never Sell Your Data

Your professional information is never traded, sold, or shared with advertisers.

No AI Training

The resumes you analyze are not used to train or improve the underlying AI models.

Your Right to be Forgotten

If you decide to stop using ApplyBuddy, you can request a full data wipe. We will delete your account history, email association, and usage stats from our Supabase database within 48 hours.
